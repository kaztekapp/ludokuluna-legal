Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. This page explains how to delete your Ludo Kuluna account and what happens to your data.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Ludo Kuluna app on your device
  2. Go to Profile (tap your profile icon in the bottom tab)
  3. Tap Settings
  4. Scroll down to Data & Storage
  5. Tap Reset Progress
  6. Confirm the deletion when prompted

Alternatively, you can request account deletion by contacting us directly at support@mkaztek.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and the email address associated with your account.

What Data Is Deleted

Data Type Action
Account credentials (email, password) Deleted
Profile (username, display name, avatar, bio) Deleted
Game statistics (wins, losses, streaks, coins) Deleted
Friends list and friend requests Deleted
Chat messages Deleted
Achievements and leaderboard entries Deleted
Subscription data Deleted (manage billing via Google Play)

Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your game progress, coins, achievements, and social connections will be permanently removed within 30 days of your request.

Retention Period

Once you request account deletion, your data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days. During this period, your account will be deactivated and inaccessible.

Active Subscriptions

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel active subscriptions. Please cancel your subscription through the Google Play Store or Apple App Store before deleting your account to avoid future charges.
