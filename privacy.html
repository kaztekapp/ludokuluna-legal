Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 3, 2026

Ludo Kuluna ("we", "our", or "us") is a mobile board game developed by KAZTEK LLC. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, username, and display name.

Game Data: We collect game statistics such as wins, losses, streaks, and coins earned.

Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for analytics and ad personalization.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the app, including features used and time spent.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the game experience
  • To manage your account and profile
  • To enable multiplayer features and matchmaking
  • To display leaderboards and friend lists
  • To show advertisements (for non-VIP users)
  • To process in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • To send push notifications (with your consent)
  • To improve the app and fix bugs

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Authentication and database hosting
  • Google AdMob: Advertising (for non-VIP users)
  • RevenueCat: Subscription and in-app purchase management
  • Google Play Services: App distribution and billing
  • Expo: App updates and notifications

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy governing the use of your information.

4. Advertising

We display ads through Google AdMob to non-VIP users. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings. VIP subscribers enjoy an ad-free experience.

5. In-App Purchases

Subscriptions are processed through Google Play. We do not directly collect or store payment information. Purchase receipts are validated through RevenueCat.

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

7. Children's Privacy

Ludo Kuluna is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Update or correct your information through the app
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising

9. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data within 30 days.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app.
